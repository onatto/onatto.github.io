Hello, I'm Onat! 

I am an enigmatic language designer, performance/UX optimization addict, graphics programming aficionado, first-principles thinker, counter-culture coder.
I've started out by making games, then moved on to making engines to make games, then languages to make engines.
I want to create games, worlds, art and music at the speed of imagination, I've been working on a dream language to achieve all that and more.
When I'm not trying to juggle too many projects simultaneously, I do handsfree cycling, dancing and improvising in jam sessions with friends.

You can contact me at onattoglu(at)gmail.com.
